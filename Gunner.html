    A Brief summary of all of Gunner's weapons

    Leadstorm

    Gunner's Leadstorm is a baisc mini-gun. Good damage,Good speed, The spread starts out bad but tightens the more you shoot it

    AutoCannon

    Gunner's Autocannon is a high powered mini gun. Great damage,Speed builds up over the duration of shooting.

    Hurricane

    Gunner's rocket launcher. Damage is good,speed is good, and you are able to control the rockets by aiming with your mouse.

    Bulldog

    Gunner's Revolver. Good damage,good speed. very reliable weapon.

    Burstfire

    Gunner's burst fire pistol. Good damage, speed is good but not the best, carrys lots of ammo which is nice.

    CoilGun

    Gunner's Magnetic Coil gun. Shoots through walls, Good damage,Speed could be better. the shoot through wall ability helps it in certain missions and cave generations.
