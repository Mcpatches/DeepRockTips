The Engineer

The Engineer's job is to help lock down areas and help provide covering fire for the team with his Sentry Guns, The engineer works and in hand with the scout since the Engineer is packing a Platform gun that allows him to fire off a platform onto any surface, This helps the scout get objectives stuck in high places with nowhere to stand. The engineers arsenal can pack a punch even without his sentrys but lacks in the movement capabilies that the other classes have.

Warthog

Engineer's Shotgun. Good damage, Speed is lacking a bit, Ammo is the main issue wit this gun.

Voltic SMG

Engineer's SMG. Good Damage, Good Speed. Pretty baisc weapon but reliable.

Smart Rifle

Engineer's "Smart" Rifle, Good damage,Good Speed, and holding down alternate fire will let you lock onto enemys in your sightline within a certain range.

Deep Core Launcher

Engineer's Grenade launcher, Great damage, Slow speed to compensate for the damage. Great for AOE and clearing swarms.

Breach Cutter

Engineer's Laser cutter, Good damage, Good Speed. Fires a little wall of three lasers which move outwards.

Shard Diffractor

Engineer's Laser Beam gun, Good damage, Speed doesn't matter like with the flamethrower due to it being a beam. This weapon relys a bit on overclocks for the best uses, but overall still good.
