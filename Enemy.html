• Mission Events

BET-C

The BET-C is the bigger version of the familar M.U.L.E, Once you enter its 15 meter range or shoot at it, BET-C wakes up and starts to attack the players. If you are able to defeat the BET-C then you can repair and have her fight along side you.

Korlock Tyrant Weed

The Tyrant weed is found randomly, once you enter its attack area. Vines pop out of the ground trying to attack you, you have to destroy the vines and wait for the bulb to open up and deal damage to the Tyrant Weed.

Nemesis

The Nemesis is a robot that mimics dwarve voice lines in caves in a static robot like voice, once the Nemesis is able to find the dwarves, it trys to grab at them with its long arms. Once defeated the Robot blows up scattering bombs all around the area.
