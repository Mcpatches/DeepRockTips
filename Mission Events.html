Mission Events

Loot Based Events

Loot based events are randomly found throughout a cave,
typically offering lots of minerals and a skin, or framework.


Enemy Based events

Enemy based events are randomly spawned either at the start, or during a mission. These can range from an exploder to a Nemesis causing the dwarves to have to put their attention on it.
