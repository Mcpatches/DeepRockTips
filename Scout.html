    A Brief summary of all of scouts weapons

    GK2

    The GK2 is scouts first primary weapon, it pretty good damage and speed. Lacks in AOE damage.

    M1000

    The M1000 is scouts sniper rifle weapon. Great damage, Slow speed. Great for single high value targets.

    Drak

    The Drak is scouts projectile launcher. Great speed,lacks in damage. Overclocks are typically what make it viable.

    Zhukov

    Scouts Zhukovs are his dual pistols. Consistently good damage and speed. overall good for most situations.

    Boomstick

    Scouts Boomstick is his shotgun. Great damage upclose, but its also a shotgun so anything other than close range it falls off very fast.

    Boltshark

    The Scouts Boltshark, crossbow with special types of arrows as secondary ammo. Good at setting different status effects. not a great way to deal fast daamge against enemys.
