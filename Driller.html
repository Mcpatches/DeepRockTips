  • The Driller

    A breif summary of all of Drillers weapons

    Flamethrower

    Driller's Flamethrower. Good damage, Range is good, Speed doesnt matter due to it being a flame infront of the gun. Can set things on fire which is helpful

    Cryo Cannon

    Driller's Cryo Cannon, works same as flamethrower but freezes enemys instead of setting them on fire. Frozen enemys take triple the normal damage.

    Sludge Pump

    Driller's Sludge Pump, damage is good, works very well with other fire based class weapons. Fire shots of sludge that can be charged for a burst of multiple piles of sludge.

    Subata

    Driller's Basic pistol. Good damage, Good speed. Very reliable weapon to use.

    EPC

    Driller's Laser gun. The EPC is defined by what upgrade you give it, either Thin Containment Field, which allows the guns charge shot to be blown up and destroy terrain, OR Burning Nightmare which lets you set things on fire the weapons normal and charged shots.

    Wave Cooker

    Driller's Radiation Gun. bad damage, Near infinite range. This weapon has very niche uses. Would not reccomend using
