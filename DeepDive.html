Deep Dives in DRG are accessible after you promote one of your dwarves, Promotions come along when you reach level 25 with that specfic character and pay a certain amount of credits and minerals depending on what Promotion level you are going to. The Deep Dives are a set of 3 missions in a row, carrying over you health and minerals between the 3. doing the deep dives nets you 3 Matrix Cores, Matrix cores are used to get Overclocks (Learn more about overclocks by clicking on it).

This weeks standard Deep Dive was in Radiocative Zone. MISSION 1: Refinery + 2 Eggs. Modifer: Lethal Enemies. MISSION 2: 4 Eggs + 150 Morkite. Modifer:Low Gravity, Haunted Cave MISSION 3: 2 Eggs + 200 Morkite. Modifer: NONE
