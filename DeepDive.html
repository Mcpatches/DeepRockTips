What is a Deep Dive?

A Deep dive is unlocked once you promote a Dwarf one time. this grants access to the Deep dive terminal which allows the gamemode to be played.
The Deep Dive gamemode consists of three missions in a row with health and nitra carrying between them and the difficulty increasing each mission.

Rewards for completing a Deep Dive.

The rewards for completing a deep dive will be the same whether it be a Elite or normal deep dive. Upon completion you will get 1 Blank matrix core which can be used on a machine event found in missions, along with 1 cosmetic and 1 weapon overclock.

Mission 2: 2 Mini Mule, Twins & Hive Modifers: Nothing
