Deep Dives

in DRG are accessible after you promote one of your dwarves, Promotions come along when you reach level 25 with that specfic character and pay a certain amount of credits and minerals depending on what Promotion level you are going to. The Deep Dives are a set of 3 missions in a row, carrying over you health and minerals between the three. doing the deep dives nets you 3 Matrix Cores, Matrix cores are used to get Overclocks.
