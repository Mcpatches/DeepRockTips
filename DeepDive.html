What is a Deep Dive?

A Deep dive is unlocked once you promote a Dwarf one time. this grants access to the Deep dive terminal which allows the gamemode to be played.
The Deep Dive gamemode consists of three missions in a row with health and nitra carrying between them and the difficulty increasing each mission.

What rewards to expect.

On each successful mission completed you are granted first a Blank core, second a Cosmetic Core, and lastly a Weapon Core.

Deep Dive in Dense Biozone

Mission 1: Twins & Hive, 2 Eggs. Modifiers: Lethal Enemys
Mission 2: Refinery, Twins. Modifers: Nothing
Mission 3: 4 Eggs, 2 Mini Mules. Modifiers: Mactera Plague

Elite Deep Dive in Crystalline Caverns

Mission 1: Refinery, Dreadnought. Modifiers: Shield Disruption
Mission 2:Twins & Dreadnought, 2 Eggs. Modifers: Swarmageddon and Low Gravity
