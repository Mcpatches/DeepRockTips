Deep Dives

in DRG are accessible after you promote one of your dwarves, Promotions come along when you reach level 25 with that specfic character and pay a certain amount of credits and minerals depending on what Promotion level you are going to. The Deep Dives are a set of three missions in a row, carrying over your health and minerals between the three. doing the deep dives nets you 3 Matrix Cores, Matrix cores are used to get Overclocks.

This Weeks Standard Deep Dive was in Fungus Bogs

Stage 1: The Twins and OG Dreadnought + Black Box MODIFER: Exploder Infestation
Stage 2: 3 Mini Mules + Twins MODIFER:NONE
Stage 3: 6 Eggs + 2 Mini Mules MODIFER: Shield Disruption
