Tips and Tricks for DRG

Tip 1

> When Depositing minerals into the M.U.L.E, Spamming the Right click and the Deposit button will make you deposit the minerals much faster than before.

Tip 2

> As the Scout, it is possible to postion yourself next to the drop pod and clip into it helping end the mission faster.

Tip 3

During the Industrial Sabotage mission, you can catch the caretakers batteries and throw them like explosives.

Tidbit 4

All the Dwarves are voiced by the same person, but the pitch is changed between them.

Tip 5

> The Engineer is able to save himself if he shoots the platform beneath himself fast enough

Tidbit 6

Sophus Alf is the composer for Deep Rock Galactic

Tip 7

Falling on a fellow dwarfs head, its possible to completely negate fall damage.

Tip 8

Holding E while on a Refinery pipe, you will automatically stop at the next build spot.

Tidbit 9

> The area the game takes place in is called "Hoxxes IV"
