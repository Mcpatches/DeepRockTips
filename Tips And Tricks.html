Tips

1. Spamming E and right click on the M.U.L.E lets you deposit faster.

2. The Scout is able to clip through the drop pod at the end of the mission to help it end faster.

3. You can catch the Caretaker batteries and use them as explosives against enemies.

4. The Engineer is able to save himself with his own platform gun.

5. Falling on a dwarves head prevents all fall damage.

6. holding E on a refinery pipe forces a stop at a build spot, or a oil spill.

7. The scout can heavily reduce fall damage if you shoot the ground with the special powder overclock.

8. The Gunner ziplines are able to be traversed faster if you jump and re-catch the zipline over and over.

Tidbits

1. All the Dwarves are voiced by one person but have the pitch changed

2. The Game takes place in "HOXXES IV"

3. Sophus Alf is the composer for Deep Rock Galactic

4. Deep Rock was released in 2018 and is now celebrating its 5th anniversary

5. You can get a custom mission control voice line by a mushroom too many times.

6. There is an acheivement if you don't kick any barrel in the space rig for 10 missions in a row.

7. The dwarves often exclaim profanitys about the man who programmed the M.U.L.E due to it malfunctioning so often.
